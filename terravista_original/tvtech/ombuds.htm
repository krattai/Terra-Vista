Ombudsman Committee

Finally, the Ombudsman Committee has been chosen. This committee is designed to serve the Terra Vista Community in the event that the residents and builders of Terra Vista are unable to reach a consensus during the development and maintainance of the community. Their various backgrounds and the respect for their work and their opinions has led the members of the community to elect them to this position. The committee, in alphabetical order:

  • Adam Gruen -
    Dr. Adam Gruen is the author of vCity 1.0, first conceptualized long before Terra Vista was born. Adam has contributed to the community before it existed with the insight and vision he has displayed in this book.
    • Adam's Credentials

  • Bernie Roehl -
    Bernie needs no introduction to many of the members of Terra Vista. He is an old-timer in the VRML community, and contributes to the growth of VRML with his writings. The experience and understanding he brings to Terra Vista will ensure the growth of this project.
    • Bernie's Credentials

  • George Philippakos -
    If anyone can be called the founder of Terra Vista, it would be George. His suggestion to the VRML mailing list on March 25, 1996, entitled "g-zip inlines and some crazy thoughts" will live forever as the foundation for the world we now live in. Since that time, George has kept the vision alive.
    • George's Credentials

  • Jeff Sonstein -
    Jeff is another old-timer in the VRML community ("since before there were browsers" in his own words), and has also contributed his writings to support the development of VRML. His experience in the computer industry and dedication to VRML has earned him the deserved respect in the VRML community and in Terra Vista.
    • Jeff's Credentials

  • Kevin Rattai -
    Kevin could be refered to as the one who has organized and rallied us together for the creation of this community. He has been extremely active in the design of Terra Vista from it's conception, and has made sure to involve everyone who is interrested. Kevin's dedication to Terra Vista is evident to all members of the community.
    • Kevin's Credentials
