Forum Ranking System


With Forum Ranking System you can add, delete or modify user ranks which will show in your forum.

There are two type of ranking system:
Global ranks
Will appear after user post a number of post. For example you create a new rank called "New User" so after his or her post past a maximum number then he or she will get a new ranks.

Special ranks
Will always assign to the user. So no matter what is his or her post, he or she will have the ranks.

When you create a new ranks, once you click on "special" then the ranks will assign min post as "-1" and max post as "-1". This way you should not worried about posting a minimum or maximum post.

P.S.: Please make sure after you create a special rank you also assign the new rank into that user by editing his or her profile using user admin.
