Garson provides the world with excellent quality limestone. I suspect the locals wonder why it is called Tyndall stone. The old quarries are a popular swimming hole for some of the braver locals. Fed by artesian wells, the water probably doesn't get much warmer than extremely cold. Garson is located 8 miles west of Beausejour on highway 44.
