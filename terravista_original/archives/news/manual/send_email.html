Send Email to User(s)


This page is menu is fairly self explanatory. Configuration options are as follows:

User Name: If you want to e-mail a specific user.
E-mail to all users: A check box to mail all users.
Reply address: The address that the e-mail will come from, i.e. webmaster@yoursite.com.
Subject: The subject of the e-mail.
Message: The main body of the e-mail.
