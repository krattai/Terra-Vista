Portal Glyphs

The glyphs depicted below are intended for use on lollyloaders, portals or any other devices for intersegment and interzone travel. These glyphs are very simple, easily rendered in either 2D or 3D. They are logical and semantically consistent, so that once you learn two or three the rest become obvious. They are also unique and unlikely to be confused with other symbols.

Glyph Name Use 
localCenter
 Go to this segment's main entrypoint. 
nextSegment
 Go to the next segment. 
nextCenter
 Go to the next segment's main entry point. 
nextZone
 Go to the next zone. 
nextZoneCenter
 Go to the next zone's main entrypoint.

Designed by Michael St. Hippolyte for the Virtual Community project. They may be freely used by anyone anywhere in the Virtual Community. Go ahead and grab 'em! Ask for other sizes and styles.
