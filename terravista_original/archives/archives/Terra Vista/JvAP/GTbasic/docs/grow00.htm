Growth of your Zone



At this point, your Zone is now planted. Now is when you want to ensure your Zone is linked up to other Zones so you will get people visiting. You may also wish to start adding features to your Zone such as flora and fauna (plants and animals), architectural wonders, and other object d'arte.

The artists, architects, and technicians at Terra Vista want to make sure there is plenty of objects available to the public to beautify these Zones. To this end, there are plenty of objects, known as mesh which are freely available from our servers. We also have a large pool of talented artists who are more than happy to show their stuff and make it available to the public. Be sure to visit our art gallery and see if there is something you may want to add to your Zone to make it unique.

You may already know how to produce VRML and you are welcome to add your talents to our pool of resources. If you do not know how, or want to learn more about how to develop within the Terra Vista environment, we have plenty of resources available to show you how. Please visit our web documents for the resources you need.

You may also wish to register your Zone with Terra Vista. By registering, your Zone will be added to a pool of Zone resources. As new Zones crop up and grow, they will need links. As such, these new Zowners may want to link to your Zone. The fun part here is to keep in contact with other Zowners and ensure your Zone will have access from many other Zones. It is all about community and sharing your wrls with your neighbors.
