Terra Vista - The Zones

  • 7 Islands by Michael St. Hippolyte
    The uncommonly cool 7 Islands puts you in the middle of a VRML ocean. A nice place for a vacation.
  • Jeremy's New Zone by Jeremy Cunningham
    Jeremy's brand new Segment B 3-Pad Bad Mamba Jamma (is it named yet?). If you can "Animate To Viewpoint", do it... cool stuff.
  • Proposed Downtown by Jay von Rosen
    My entry into the zones, an attempt to be a central location for government buildings. Barely functional right now, but big plans are in the works.
  • The Flux by Matthew Muntean
    A VRML "Index" for Terra Vista. Very likely the "Virtual Peanut Butter" that binds us together.
  • George Towne by George Phillipakos
    Our founder's entry to Terra Vista. I believe this is the original Terra Vista zone.
  • Outland by Besjon Alivandi
    Webking Besjon, better known to Terra Vistans as "CGI-VRML King", has given us this zone which is created by a Perl script on the fly.
  • Talosian Spacestation by Len Bullard
    Mr. Live3D-Spin-Node himself has created this space station, making use of frames and music for a more enjoyable experience. Immerse yourself into Len's VRML Fiction, and explore every aspect of the unfolding story.
  • The Wessex Internet Zone by Alexander Veenendaal
    Our first commercial entity, Wessex is being provided for their customers as a link-zone to their web pages.
  • Uvea by Kevin Rattai
    Kevin's new segment, reminiscent of the wide open spaces of the Great White North. C'mon up and wander around a while, eh?
