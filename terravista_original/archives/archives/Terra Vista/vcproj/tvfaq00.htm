Just The FAQs




Q.  What is Terra Vista?

A.  Terra Vista is a colaborative VRML environment.

Q.  Where is Terra Vista?

A.  Some say Terra Vista is in "CyberSpace".  Some of us believe it
_IS_ Cyberspace.  Terra Vista exists on the internet.

Q.  Why is Terra Vista?

A.  When Terra Vista began, it was agreed a large portion of what was
missing in VRML was large, linked, navagable VRML environments.  We
are striving to create exactly that.

Q.  How to join the Terra Vista list?

A. To subscribe to the list send email to:

majordomo@terravista.org

with the body:

subscribe village

As a result of this, you will get a message asking you to confirm your
subscription request by sending another mail to majordomo. It will
contain a line of text that must be in the the body of the message.
The mail itself will include more information and instructions, so
just read and follow.

To unsubscribe, send email to

majordomo@terrvista.org

with the mesage body

unsubscribe village

That's it!


Q.  Who can participate?

A.  Anyone is welcome to participate within Terra Vista.  It is not
exclusive.  One of our goals is to create guidelines for creating
environments with the Terra Vista model which are easy to duplicate.

Q.  Who does participate?

A.  People from many different industry sectors participate in Terra
Vista.  From graphic artists, historians, programmers, engineers.  We
even have the Hospitality Industry participating.

Q.  What do I need to visit Terra Vista?

A.  A computer, and internet connection, and a VRML browser or plugin
for an HTML browser.  Certainly, the faster the computer, the better
the results.

Q.  Can I get a domain name and address in Terra Vista?

A.  Yes you can.  A Zone can be given a dynamic terravista.org domain
name.  And, you can get a personal email address @terravista.org.

Q.  How do I build for Terra Vista?

A.  To best answer that question, go to the Terra Vista technical
pages and read the tutorials.  And, if you have any further questions,
ask the list and we will help out.

Q.  I've heard there are a lot of restrictions, is this true?

A.  Early in the Terra Vista development, restrictions on certain
Zones were created by the Zowners.  There was some concern about files
being too large and large computers required.  Any restrictions there
may have been do not apply to everyone.  Build to your own
specifications as a Zowner.

Q.  How do I contribute to Terra Vista?

A.  Become a member, participate on the list serve, add links to Terra
Vista on your web space, create mesh or ask to contribute on an
ongoing project, write articles for the Verbal.  There are many ways
to contribute, everyone's input is welcome.

Q.  I built my world, but I don't have any content in it.  How do I
get some?

A.  An excellent way to get content on your Zone is to ask for
contributions from the list members.  We are also slowly archiving
mesh contributed to the community by past and current members. 
Anything from flora to fauna, urban to suburban objects.  And the
community always welcomes more contributions for mesh.
