Ombudsman Exposition - Bernie Roehl

Ombudsman Candidates 

I am Bernie Roehl.  I do software development and system administration

for the Electrical and Computer Engineering department of the University

of Waterloo in Ontario, Canada.  I'm the co-author of "Using VRML"

(recently published by Que) and a regular columnist for Virtual Reality

Special Report, VR News and WebSMITH.  I've authored two other books on

virtual reality, and am probably best known in VR circles for REND386

and AVRIL (two freeware VR libraries for the PC platform).



I got into VRML because of my prior interest in VR.



I'm interested in helping to design and construct one of the first true

virtual communities.  My main area of contribution will likely be on the

technical end of things.
