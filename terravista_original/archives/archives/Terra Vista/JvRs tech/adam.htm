Ombudsman Exposition - Adam Gruen

Ombudsman Candidates 



Adam Gruen is the author of vCity, 1.0.

http://www.well.com/user/adamgru/vCity



Dr. Gruen is a historian of technology with specialties in the

history of aerospace and civil and structural engineering.  He

received his Ph.D. from Duke University in 1989.  In 1984 he

moved from Durham N.C. to Washington, D.C. on a Guggenheim

fellowship at the (U.S.) National Air & Space Museum, and

stayed in the area ever since.  From 1985-1993 he served as

Director of NASA's Space Station History Project; in February

1994, he began working for MCI as their corporate historian.



His interest in VRML was sparked in autumn 1994 while doing

research on the history of the Internet.  In late 1994, he began

writing the vCity novel and finished it in August 1995.



Dr. Gruen's interest in helping guide the development of terra

vista is not purely altruistic.  He figures this is history in

the making, and he intends to write the definitive (!) history

of these pioneering days before any other professional historian

does.
