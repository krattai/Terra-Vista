vCity 1.0, The Novel Concept
by Adam L. Gruen

vCity 1.0, The Novel Concept is a webwork that adapts the original 1995 novel to the web.

vCity 1.0, The Novel Concept has more than merely the original text; it offers the reader/viewer a complete table of contents (called META), additional commentary on each chapter (called MIDRASH), appendices, and an index. At the bottom of each page you'll find buttons that will take you to other places in the webwork.

As time marches on, I and others will be adding to this webwork with additional commentary supplied by reader/viewers as well as image maps and VRML illustrations. Checking the META page or the vCity satellite map is a good way to catch up on the latest additions.

A completely new addition is the vCity: Tales section. For those of you who want your vCity stories one episode at a time.

If you have any suggestions or comments please send them by e-mail to adamgru@well.com.
