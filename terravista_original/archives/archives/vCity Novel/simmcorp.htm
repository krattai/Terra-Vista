Registry of Simmcorps

Words of introduction

A simmcorp is a simulated corporation which comprises part of the vCity simulated economy. Below you will find an alphabetical list of all the simmcorps in existence, except for those we haven't listed yet because we're pretty busy, you know?

In many of the descriptions of simmcorps you will see irritating paranthetical expressions such as [vTmk] or [vP]. These stand for vTrademark and vPatent, respectively. It should be obvious that these designations do not mean that the actual words and technologies have been trademarked or patented in any national jurisdiction. We like doing it this way because it cuts down on paperwork. Anyway, if, by some weird chance, one of the names or descriptions you peruse herein happens to be depressingly similar to something in the real world (or even worse an accidental duplication of a real trademark) and if this upsets you, please let us know. We can work things out; that's life in a peaceful anarchy.
