SynapsetV


SynapsetV opened its doors on December 1, 1996

SynapsetV has gone through some changes and is pleased to announce a continuum of life.

We are entering a new millennia! As we do so, our technologies for social interaction and interaction with the machine get better along the way. We now have many products and technologies for participating in workgroups from remote locations, one of which is known as VRML and uses the internet as its backbone. SynapsetV is not intended to focus on any one tool or function, but is set out to give anyone a voice for where they see these technologies leading us. Virtual Reality is not quite available to the general public, but through the use of VRML for the SynapsetV events, the general public can at last experience what the future holds in store.

A rotation of events will take place in the SynapsetV forum on a fairly regular basis. At this time, no events are scheduled. As events begin to take place, they will be listed here on this page.

Unfortunately, one of the events which was to take place on December 7, 1996 has been postponed until further notice. I do appologize of any inconvenience this may have caused anyone.

We are currently looking for more content for the events in the form of topics for presentation or for panel discussion. If you would like to do a presentation or would like to participate in a panel, please contact me to discuss this further. Also, we would like to have a number of physical groups to get together. Locally in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada we will be holding a physical group participation for demonstrations and would like to invite other groups to join in a similar fashion.

For further information and to discuss participation, please contact me via email response.

Thank you,

Kevin Rattai
